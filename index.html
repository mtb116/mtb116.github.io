PORTFOLIO

Learning Over Quality: Enabling students to create compelling and authentic portfolio projects.

Portfolio projects can help faculty identify gaps in curriculum; where student performance does not align with desired outcomes. Additionally, portfolio projects allow students to assess and reflect on their own learning. However, these advantages are lost if beginner level portfolio projects are graded for quality.

To increase equity and meaningful learning opportunities for students, while still supporting organizational outcomes, this document aims to persuade faculty at coding schools to adopt grading strategies inspired by labor-based grading techniques.
